bce-simulation

bce-simulation is a set of simulations written for a research project regarding Buffon’s Needle and Coin problems as well as our variations. It serves to verify theoretical findings through experimental methods.

Program

Simulation

The Coin Simulation runs Buffon’s Coin problem and shows the probability of a coin crossing one of the lines of a square grid.

The Coin Simulation (Variation) runs our variation of Buffon’s Coin problem with additional calculations: it detects if the coin can really balance with additional calculations, and shows the probability of the coin balancing on one of the lines of a square grid.

The Needle Simulation runs Buffon’s Needle problem and shows the probability of a needle crossing one of the lines of two parallel lines.

The Needle Simulation (Variation) runs our variation of Buffon’s Needle problem with additional calculations: it detects if the needle can really balance with additional calculations, and shows the probability of a needle balancing one of the lines of two parallel lines.
